Nintendo Game Series

Super Mario

Super Mario is one of the most iconic video game franchises, known for its platforming gameplay and beloved characters.

The Legend of Zelda

The Legend of Zelda series is a mix of action, adventure, and puzzle-solving, offering players a journey through vast and imaginative worlds.

Splatoon

Splatoon is a unique, fast-paced multiplayer shooter where players control squid-like characters to cover the map in ink.

Kirby

Kirby centers around his adventures as he fights to protect and save his home on the distant Planet Popstar from a variety of threats.
