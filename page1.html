The Story of Nintendo

Nintendo was founded in 1889 as a playing card company in Kyoto, Japan, by Fusajiro Yamauchi. It gradually evolved into a major player in the video game industry, creating iconic characters like Mario and Zelda, and innovative consoles like the NES and Nintendo Switch.
